Our services

Here are a few things we offer that might interest you

VOICE TRAINING

We believe each singer is gifted uniquely. Delhi vocal coaching strives to bring the individuality and uniqueness of each voice by offering individual classes with coaches who focus on the individual’s unique talent and voice category. The coaching process is tailor made to suit the uniquie needs and the ability of the each student.

MUSIC THEORY

The students are encouraged to learn Music theory and sight singing as part of their vocal classes. It will be a group class of once in 2 weeks. This provides them with the foundation to write and read music.

MUSIC ENSEMBLE

We encourage our students to learn and appreciate harmony. Generally Choral singing is facilitated through special sessions.

HISTORY OF MUSIC

Special sessions for interested pupil are provided for students to learn in-depth the background and history of the music.

About us

“To bring meaning and life through music”

Delhi Vocal Coaching

DVC is a home-based vocal coaching specializing in western classical music that provides professional vocal coaching to singers of all ages at affordable rates. It seeks to provide all singers the necessary tools and techniques required to master the art of singing and help them become the best they can be in their vocal expression.

To build a community of singers who can support and encourage each other in the city of Delhi.

To serve the community, city, and country at large through the service of singing.

Rates

Helping you to take the next step

Delhi Vocal Coaching offers a variable scale for voice lessons. The Sliding Scale is designed to help singers avail financial flexibility for their professional vocal coaching. You may decide the rate that is most appropriate for your financial situation. Your rate for lessons is a personal choice with no questions asked. You may alter what you pay at any time as your financial situation changes.

Singers with lower income, pay at the lower end of the sliding scale while higher-income students pay the high end. Singers with moderate income pay the average rate or somewhere middle that best suits their financial condition.

The student can choose to pay per session or monthly. The charges are as follows:

600-900₹Per Session

  • 45 mins per Session
  • For Admission 100₹

2200-3600₹Per Month

  • 4 Session in a Month
  • For Admission 100₹

POLICIES

  • Each session is for 45 minutes.
  • There will be 4 sessions in a month.
  • If a month has 5 weeks, regular classes will not be conducted in the last week.
  • However if a student wishes to have a special class in the last week, they may do so depending on the availability of the teacher with an additional payment of 800 Rupees.
  • The last week is only for make-up classes and special appointments.
  • The fees must be paid prior to 7th of each month else a late fee of INR 100 would be levied for every week delayed.
  • If it is a stand-alone session then the payment may be made when fixing the appointment or right after the session.
