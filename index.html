Our services

Here are a few things we offer that might interest you in

VOICE TRAINING

We believe each singer is gifted uniquely. Delhi vocal coaching strives to bring the individuality and uniqueness of each voice by offering individual classes with coaches who focus on the individual’s unique talent and voice category. The coaching process is tailor made to suit the uniquie needs and the ability of the each student.

MUSIC THEROY

The students are encouraged to learn Music theory and sight singing as part of their vocal classes. It will be a group class of once in 2 weeks. This provides them with the foundation to write and read music.

MUSIC ENSEMBLE

We encourage our students to learn and appreciate harmony. Generally Choral singing is facilitated through special sessions.

HISTORY OF MUSIC

Special sessions for interested pupil are provided for students to learn in-depth the background and history of the music.

About us

“To bring meaning and life through music”

Delhi Vocal Coaching

DVC is a home-based vocal coaching specializing in western classical music that provides professional vocal coaching to singers of all ages at affordable rates. It seeks to provide all singers the necessary tools and techniques required to master the art of singing and help them become the best they can be in their vocal expression.

To build a community of singers who can support and encourage each other in the city of Delhi.

To serve the community, city, and country at large through the service of singing.
